FP&A Framework

Why FP&A dashboards fail executive adoption

Many dashboards fail because they are built as reporting tools, not decision systems. Executives usually need three things: what changed, why it changed, and what action should be taken.

Framework

  • Start with the business decision, not the dataset.
  • Build a clear metric hierarchy: revenue, margin, cost, cash, and strategic KPIs.
  • Separate diagnostic pages from executive summary pages.
  • Use commentary to explain variance, drivers, risk, and next steps.

A good FP&A dashboard should reduce meeting time, improve alignment, and make trade-offs visible.
