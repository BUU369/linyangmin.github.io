AI for Finance

AI workflow ideas for finance teams

AI can help finance teams move faster, but it should not replace financial judgment. The best use cases combine structured data, business context, and human review.

Useful use cases

  • Drafting variance commentary from actuals, forecast, and prior-year data.
  • Summarizing management review materials into executive narratives.
  • Generating scenario questions for forecast review meetings.
  • Creating first-draft PowerPoint storylines from financial analysis.

The principle is simple: automate the first draft, not the final decision.
